Research Interests

Areas of interest and thematic directions of my academic work.

My research interests lie in pure mathematics, with a primary focus on number theory. Within number theory, I am drawn to questions related to transcendence, Diophantine problems, and the interaction of number theoretic functions with analytic methods. I am also interested in related areas of algebra and analysis that naturally connect to these themes.

I am broadly motivated by foundational and conceptual aspects of mathematics, and I am keen on developing a deeper understanding of abstract structures and their underlying principles.

  • Transcendental number theory
  • Diophantine approximation
  • Analytic methods in number theory
  • Connections with algebra and analysis
